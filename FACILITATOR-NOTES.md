# Facilitator Notes (starter)

Suggested run sequence:
1. Choose scenario archetype and calibrate domain weights (w) and coupling (λ)
2. Inspect propagation map by domain and click nodes for instrument readouts
3. Step through escalation timeline and IR workflow lanes
4. Record decisions + thresholds; generate an After-Action Review (AAR) externally or via export (if implemented)
5. Debrief: identify governance breakpoints, dependencies, and mitigation actions
